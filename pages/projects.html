Why join Founders and Coders Academy (FAC) Skills Bootcamp programme.

Software development as part of the information and technology sphere has played a significant role in developing web applications through web development using web technologies, i.e. HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have always wanted to become a web developer and looking forward to joining the Founders and Coders Skills Bootcamp programme to become a professional web developer.

Becoming a renowned programmer has become a priority for me since the year 2016. After a successful simulation and analyzing the water quality of a particular watershed in the Upper Ebonyi River watershed using ArcGis (Geographic Information System) and SWAT (Soil and Water Assessment tool). I became interested in developing software applications for solving real-life problems.

I decided to focus on learning how to develop a web application since web-based applications has tremendously changed the face of the World Wide Web (WWW) and have helped solve some challenges facing our world today. For instance, Uber, Whatsapp, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google map and Twitter has actually improved human life in all spheres.

My journey to becoming a full-stack web developer started in 2018 with Andela's learning community. Through that program, I learnt about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and a few reactjs. I built small web applications using the skills I learnt during the program.

Furthermore, I was opportune to also participated in another Andela's learning community sponsored by Facebook using OpenClassRoom as learning platform, this program was called facebook development circle. At the end of the program I was able to secure a job as a junior developer with a step-up company in Lagos Nigeria.

Furthermore, the reason why I want to join FAC after my research on various internet sources on FAC reviews on switchup I found out it will be a great opportunity to develop my skill-set further with FAC bootcamp. I strongly believe that with FAC bootcamp I will transform myself and my team to senior developer within a few months.

Finally, Apart from my research on the web about FAC, the first online meet-up that was held on 20th November 2022 on Zoom platform was astonishing. I was able to hear from the Host Dan and Sarah about FAC and the success rate of the applicants in Landing a software development job after their Apprenticeship position. I would not say I am surprised about their comments that about 12 applicants in some occasion 11 or 7 applicants secure job opportunity, such comments reaffirm my stands about joining FAC bootcamp.
