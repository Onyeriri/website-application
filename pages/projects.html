Why join Founders and Coders Academy (FAC) Skills Bootcamp programme.

Becoming a renowned programmer has become a priority to me since the year 2016. After a successful simulation and analyzing water quality of a particular watershed in Upper Ebonyi River watershed using ArcGis (Geographic Information System) and SWAT (Soil and water Assessment tool). I became interested in developing such software application for solving real-life problems.

I made a decision to focus in learning how to develop a web application since web based application's has tremendously change the face of World Wide Web (WWW) and have actually helped solve some challenges facing our world today. For instance Uber, Whatsapp, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google map and Twitter has actually improved human life in all sphere's.

My journey to become a fullstack web developer started in the year 2018 with Andela's learning community. Through that program I learnt about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and a little bit of reactjs. I was able to build small web application using the skills I learnt during the program.

Furthermore, I was opportune to also participated in another Andela's learning community sponsored by Facebook using OpenClassRoom as learning platform, this program was called facebook development circle. At the end of the program I was able to secure a job as a junior developer with a step-up company in Lagos Nigeria.

Finally, the reason why I want to join FAC after my research on various internet sources on FAC reviews for instance switchup I found out it will be a great opportunity to develop my skill-set further with FAC bootcamp. I strongly believe that with FAC bootcamp I will transform myself and my team to senior developer within a few months. Apart from my research on the web about FAC, the first online meet-up that was held on 20th November 2022 with Zoom platform was astonishing. I was able to hear from the Host Dan and Sarah about FAC and the success rate of the applicants in Landing a software development job after their Apprenticeship position. I would not say I am surprised on their comments that about 12 applicants in some occasion 11 or 7 applicants secure job opportunity, such comments reaffirm my stands about joining FAC bootcamp.
