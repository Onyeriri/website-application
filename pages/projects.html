Why join Founders and Coders Skills Bootcamp programme.

Becoming a renowned programmer has become a priority to me since the year 2016. After a successful simulation and analyzing water quality of a particular watershed in Upper Ebonyi River watershed ArcGis (Geographic Information System) and SWAT (Soil and water Assessment tool). I became interested in developing such software application for solving real-life problems. I made a decision to focus in learning how to develop a web application since web based application's has tremendously change the face of World Wide Web (WWW) and have actually helped solve some challenges facing our world today. For instance Uber, Whatsapp, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google map and Twitter has actually improved human life in all sphere's.

My journey to become a fullstack web developer started in the year 2018 with Andela's learning community. Through that program I learnt about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and a little bit of reactjs. I was able to build small web application using the skills I learnt during the program.

Furthermore, I was opportune to also participated in another Andela's learning community sponsored by Facebook, this program was called facebook development circle. At the end of the program I was able to secure a job as a junior developer with a stepup company in Lagos Nigeria.
