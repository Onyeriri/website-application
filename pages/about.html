Background

I was born and raised in Nigeria in a family of five including my parents. I grew up in a town called Onitsha, Anambra state in South-Eastern Nigeria. While growing up I found out that most South-Eastern Nigeria has good soil type and condition suitable for agriculture. This fact influenced my interest of becoming agricultural Engineer to help mechanize our old farming systems that required traditional farming tools i.e using hoes, shovels and match. In the year 2014 I graduated from the University of Nigeria Nsukka.
